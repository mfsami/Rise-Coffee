about us

our story

Rise Coffee began as a pop-up espresso cart in 2019, run by Jenny & Mark who believed mornings deserved better. What started with a borrowed machine and a handful of regulars has grown into a community hub where great coffee meets genuine connection.

our values

community first

Coffee is more than a drink; it’s a way to bring people together.

care over pretension

We source responsibly and roast with care, but keep the vibe laid-back and welcoming.

sustainability always

From cups to direct-trade partnerships, we believe every choice matters.

events at rise

Looking for a space with good energy (and even better coffee)? Our café is available for community gatherings, art shows, live music, workshops, and private events. Whether you’re planning an intimate reading or a lively launch party, we’ve got the space — and, of course, the coffee.
